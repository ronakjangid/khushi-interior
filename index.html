About Us

We are a modern interior design studio based in Ahmedabad, creating functional and elegant spaces for homes, offices, and commercial projects across India.

The studio is led by Rakesh Jangid, who continues his family's legacy in interior craftsmanship that spans over 50 years. With more than 20 years of experience in the field, he brings a deep understanding of design, execution, and client needs to every project.

Every project is handled with care, creativity, and attention to detail. No hidden costs. No last-minute surprises. Just honest work and lasting results.
